About the Arthur Blair Film Festival:

The Arthur Blair Film Festival is an annual film festival hosted on April 27th, each year at a secret location in Waterloo, Canada.

The festival is used to highlight independent productions which take a unique approach to narration and storytelling, while focusing on engaging audiences. The location of the festival is announced annually on April 1st, 27 days prior to screening.

2025 Festival: hosted at 43.48545° N, 80.51805° W, Waterloo Ontario

2024 Festival: hosted at 43.28230° N, 80.31477° W, Waterloo Ontario

2023 Festival: hosted at 43°28274° N, 80.31270° W, Waterloo Ontario
