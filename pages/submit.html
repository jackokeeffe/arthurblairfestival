Submit to the Arthur Blair Film Festival:

Guidelines:

  • Films must be under 20 minutes.
  • International submissions are allowed.
  • By submitting you allow the screening of your film at the 2026 festival.
  • All films must be submitted by March 31st, 2026.

Send us a pitch document containing the following:

  • Logline: a short quote from the film (1 - 10 words)
  • Synopsis (10 - 50 words)
  • Director's Statement (50 - 200 words)
  • A link to the film (& password if required)
